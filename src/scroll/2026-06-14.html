Welcome

We Are Glad You Are Here!

We are honored that you decided to join us this morning. Whether it is your first time in church, your first time in a long while, or part of your regular rhythm, we hope you will be encouraged by the fellowship with each other, and with our Lord, as we worship him together.

If this is your first time with us, please take a moment and fill out a Next Steps Card found on the back of the pew. We would love to meet you and help you get connected.

Children & Nursery

Children’s Church — Children worship corporately with the congregation and are dismissed for Children’s Church following the offering and choral anthem. Children ages 4 through 5th grade meet in the Fellowship Hall, located at the northeast corner of the church campus. The Youth Group is leading Children’s Church during the month of June. Nursery rooms for infants through age 3 are in the Education Wing, with the entrance behind the playground on the west side of campus. Children are always welcome in worship!

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the power of the gospel, rooted in God’s Word and the Reformed tradition, in order to create a culture of discipleship at FPC where disciples WORSHIP holistically, GROW relationally in Christ, and SERVE boldly in the world for the glory of God. Our mission is to accomplish this by “Being Disciples and Making Disciples.”

Corporate Prayer of Confession

God, our heavenly Father, You raised Jesus from death to life and crowned Him Lord of all. Yet, we confess that we regularly enthrone ourselves as king. Forgive our rebel hearts and treasonous ways, for we are those who regularly set our minds on earthly things instead of things above where Christ is. May Your Spirit lead us again to boast in the cross so that we might possess a sincere, pure, and undistracted devotion to our Lord, for we pray in the name of the One who possesses all authority in heaven and on earth, Jesus Christ our Lord.
